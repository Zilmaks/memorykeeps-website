About

MemoryKeeps is a nonprofit platform for preserving the life stories of elders — in their own voices, with their own photographs, on their own time.

We believe memory is the most precious thing we leave behind. Others sell the printing; we give the printing as a gift. The real work — and the real gift — is the listening.

Operated under FMedu, a federally registered nonprofit since 2017, our platform hosts stories from seniors served by partner organizations across communities. Free for every senior. Private by default. Preserved for generations.
